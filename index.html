    Stacy Phillips is a freelance fashion stylist, brand consultant and image expert who has helped many of the most high profile names in the fashion industry launch lines and connect with audiences via television and multimedia platforms. Among the numerous brands and designers she has collaborated with include Isaac Mizrahi, Bob Mackie, Clinton Kelly, Lori Goldstein, Dennis Basso, Jennifer Hudson, Rachel Zoe, Heidi Klum, Nicole Richie and the Kardashians.
